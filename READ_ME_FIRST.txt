HOW TO START
1. Extract the ZIP file.
2. Open the folder.
3. Double-click START_HERE.html.
4. Login.

DEFAULT LOGINS
Admin: admin / admin123
Teacher: teacher / teacher123

IMPORTANT O-LEVEL MARKS ENTRY UPDATE
For S1-S4 teachers:
- Open Teacher Subject Marks.
- Enter the raw mark scored by the learner.
- Enter what the assessment was marked out of.
- The system automatically converts each T1-T5 score to /3.
- The report card automatically calculates the formative score out of 20.
- Use X or - when the learner missed an assessment.

A-LEVEL
S5-S6 still use C1-C5 scores with marked-out-of values and A-E grading.
