STANDARD SMS GUIDE

1. LEVELS
- S1-S4: O-Level, streams A, B, C, no combinations.
- S5-S6: A-Level, Arts and Sciences, no streams.

2. TEACHER ACCESS
- Admin creates teacher users from School Users.
- Assign each teacher a subject and class/section.
- When the teacher logs in, only the assigned subject/class marks page opens.
- Teachers cannot view report cards, fees, admissions, or school settings.

3. O-LEVEL MARKS ENTRY
For S1-S4, teachers enter:
- T1 Score and T1 Out of
- T2 Score and T2 Out of
- T3 Score and T3 Out of
- T4 Score and T4 Out of
- T5 Score and T5 Out of
The system converts raw marks to /3 using: raw score / marked out of × 3.
The O-Level report card displays the converted T1-T5 scores out of 3 and calculates the formative score out of 20.

4. MISSED ASSESSMENT
- Enter X or - for a missed assessment.
- The subject will show X on the O-Level report card.
- The comment becomes Missed Assessment.

5. A-LEVEL MARKS ENTRY
For S5-S6, teachers enter C1-C5 score and marked-out-of values.
The system calculates the weight, grade, descriptor, total points, and comments.

6. BACKUP
Use Backup to save your data as JSON.
